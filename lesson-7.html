Swedish at the Doctor — Health & Symptoms

Describe how you feel, book a vårdcentral appointment, and understand what the doctor tells you.

Expat in Sweden  ·  12 min read
📚 Swedish for Expats — Course Progress
Lesson 7 of 10  ·  70% done — the finish line is in sight!
👋 From my experience: Swedish healthcare is excellent but the system is different from many countries. You go to a vårdcentral (health centre) first, not a hospital. Most doctors speak English, but receptionists may not — and the phone booking system is entirely in Swedish. This lesson is one of the most practical in the whole course.

Time to complete: 12–15 minutes  ·  Words learned: Body parts + 15 health phrases

Describing Symptoms

SwedishEnglish
Jag mår inte bra.I don't feel well.
Jag har ont i huvudet.I have a headache.
Jag har ont i halsen.I have a sore throat.
Jag har ont i magen.I have a stomach ache.
Jag har feber.I have a fever.
Jag är förkyld.I have a cold.
Jag är trött.I am tired.
Jag har hosta.I have a cough.
Det gör ont här.It hurts here.
Sedan [number] dagar.For [number] days.
💡 Key pattern: "Jag har ont i [body part]" = I have pain in [body part]. This pattern works for almost any body pain. Learn the body parts above and you can describe almost any symptom.

🌟 Expat essential: Call 1177 any time you are unsure if you need a doctor. Nurses give free medical advice. Many speak English. It is one of Sweden's best services and most expats don't know about it.

At the Vårdcentral — A Conversation

Lahiru: Hej, jag skulle vilja boka en tid hos en läkare.
Hello, I would like to book an appointment with a doctor.
Receptionist: Vad gäller det?
What is it regarding?
Lahiru: Jag mår inte bra. Jag har ont i halsen och feber sedan tre dagar.
I don't feel well. I have had a sore throat and fever for three days.
Receptionist: Vi har en ledig tid på torsdag klockan tio. Passar det?
We have a free slot on Thursday at ten o'clock. Does that work?
Lahiru: Ja, det passar bra. Tack!
Yes, that works fine. Thank you!
New words: boka = to book · ledig tid = free slot · passar det = does that work? · det passar bra = that works well
