Getting Around — Transport & Directions in Swedish

Bus, train, metro, taxi — navigate Sweden's excellent public transport system with the right Swedish words.

Expat in Sweden  ·  10 min read
📚 Swedish for Expats — Course Progress
Lesson 6 of 10  ·  60% done — you are well on your way!
👋 From my experience: Sweden has outstanding public transport — especially in Stockholm. But the apps, signs, and announcements are all in Swedish. Once I learned the key transport words, commuting went from stressful to simple. The tunnelbana (metro) is actually a joy to use when you understand what's being announced.

💡 Stockholm tip: Get the SL app (Stockholm's public transport). Everything is in Swedish but very visual. The word to know: Hållplats = stop/station. And Nästa hållplats = next stop — you'll hear this on every bus and tram.

Asking for Directions — A Conversation

Lahiru: Ursäkta, var är tunnelbanan?
Excuse me, where is the metro?
Passer-by: Gå rakt fram, sedan sväng till höger vid det röda huset.
Go straight ahead, then turn right at the red house.
Lahiru: Hur långt är det?
How far is it?
Passer-by: Ungefär fem minuter till fots.
About five minutes on foot.
Lahiru: Tack så mycket!
Thank you very much!
Passer-by: Varsågod! Lycka till!
You're welcome! Good luck!
New words: vid = at/by · till fots = on foot · ungefär = approximately · lycka till = good luck
