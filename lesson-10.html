What to Do Next

This course gave you a strong foundation. Here is how to keep growing:

📱

Duolingo Swedish

Free, 5 minutes a day. Great for building vocabulary and daily habit. Use it alongside real-life practice.

🎧

Swedish Podcasts

Try "Swedish Pod 101" or "Slow Swedish" on Spotify. Listening daily is the fastest way to improve naturally.

📺

SVT Play

Sweden's free public TV streaming service. Watch Swedish shows with Swedish subtitles — the best immersion tool available.

🏫

SFI — Svenska för invandrare

Free Swedish classes for immigrants, run by your municipality. Sign up as soon as you are registered in Sweden. It's fully funded.

💬

Tandem / HelloTalk

Language exchange apps. Find a Swedish speaker who wants to learn your language and practise together for free.

📖

This Blog

More lessons are coming — intermediate vocabulary, Swedish grammar basics, and expat culture guides. Subscribe to get notified!

💡 Most important advice: Don't wait until you're "ready" to speak Swedish. There is no ready. Speak badly, get corrected gently, improve. Every Swedish person you speak to is rooting for you.
